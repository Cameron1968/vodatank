Our Products

General Description

VodaTank is a single-walled water bottle made of stainless steel, it measured 26cm (roughly 10 inches) in height and 7.5cm (3 inches) in diameter, it was scaled down 3.5:1 from a standard 11 liters scuba diving cylinder. It fits perfectly into your palm. The body of the bottle is finished with glossy protective layer, creating a shiny and blinking effect especially under sunlight.

Product Materials

  • Bottle cap: food grade acrylonitrile butadiene styrene (ABS), food grade paint
  • Bottle cap decorative knob: thermoplastic polyurethane (TPU)
  • Bottle body: austenite 750 stainless steel
  • Bottle cap seal: food grade silicone
  • Bootle boot: thermoplastic polyurethane (TPU)
